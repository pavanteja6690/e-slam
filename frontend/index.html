About

A few years after passing out, memories of college come back to you taking you down the memory lane. A few years ago Slam Books were a great tool to reminisce "the good old" days, unfortunately the digital age has replaced the culture of writing Slams. While we understand that there can not be a better experience than carefully flippimg those brown old pages, and having a good laugh at all the stuff you did at the college. The pandemic has snatched that oppertunity away from the Class of 2020. And this is our humble effort to create a Virtual Slam book for the Class of 2020.
